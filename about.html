About Thailand Motorsports Tourism

Your trusted gateway to Thailand's thrilling motorsports scene. Since 2020, we've been connecting racing enthusiasts from around the world with unforgettable motorsports experiences in the Land of Smiles.

🎯

Our Mission

To make Thailand the world's premier motorsports tourism destination by providing exceptional racing experiences that combine world-class motorsports with authentic Thai culture, creating memories that last a lifetime.

🌟

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as the leading expert in Thailand motorsports tourism, known for our exceptional service, insider access, and commitment to delivering authentic racing experiences that exceed expectations.

Our Story

Thailand Motorsports Tourism was born from a passion for racing and a love for Thailand's incredible culture. Founded by motorsports enthusiasts who recognized Thailand's potential as a world-class racing destination, we began with a simple mission: to share the excitement of Thai motorsports with the world.

What started as a small team organizing circuit visits has grown into Thailand's most trusted motorsports tourism company. We've witnessed the transformation of Thailand's racing scene - from the historic Bira Circuit to the modern Chang International Circuit, and now the upcoming Bangkok F1 Grand Prix 2028.

Today, we work with international racing fans, travel agencies, and motorsports organizations to create unforgettable racing experiences that showcase both Thailand's motorsports excellence and its rich cultural heritage.
