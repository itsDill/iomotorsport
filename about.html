About iomotorsport

Understanding the business forces that drive modern motorsport and shape the future of racing worldwide.

Our Mission

iomotorsport exists to fill a critical gap in motorsport journalism: comprehensive business intelligence. While millions follow the on-track action, few understand the financial and strategic decisions that determine success or failure.

We analyze the business side of racing—team valuations, sponsorship economics, manufacturer investments, and the corporate strategies shaping modern motorsport. Our analysis is data-driven, transparent, and built for audiences who want to understand the money behind the machines.

Why This Matters

  • Understand team valuations and why Ferrari is worth billions
  • Get insights into manufacturer motorsport strategies
  • Track sponsorship trends and what brands are investing in
  • Follow mergers, acquisitions, and business restructures in racing
  • Learn how regulatory changes impact team economics and viability
  • Predict which teams will thrive or struggle in the years ahead

What We Cover

Our editorial focus spans the most significant racing series where serious corporate and financial interests are at stake.

Formula 1

Team valuations, powertrain manufacturer investments, title sponsorship deals, and the multi-billion dollar economic model of modern F1.

NASCAR

Charter valuations, broadcast economics, corporate team ownership structures, and how manufacturer programs drive business decisions.

IMSA & Endurance Racing

Manufacturer participation economics, OEM investment strategies, sponsorship models, and the business case for hypercar programs.

World Rally Championship & Offroad

Manufacturer competition strategies, privateer economics, sponsorship structures, and growth opportunities in emerging markets.

Our Coverage Areas

We organize our analysis into key business domains that shape motorsport:

Valuations

Comprehensive team asset valuations, franchise worth analysis, and comparable market assessments

Deals & M&A

Mergers, acquisitions, ownership changes, and strategic partnerships reshaping race teams

Economics

Budget caps, cost controls, revenue sharing, and financial viability of teams and series

Sponsorships

Title deals, major partnerships, brand value analysis, and ROI for corporate motorsport investments

Manufacturers

OEM investment strategies, powertrain programs, and corporate racing initiatives

Technology

Technical regulations, platform economics, and how innovation drives competitive and financial advantage

Our Approach

Data-Driven Analysis

Every valuation, figure, and claim is sourced from public filings, verified reports, or transparent methodologies. We clearly distinguish between confirmed data, industry estimates, and analysis.

Independent Perspective

We're not affiliated with teams, series, broadcasters, or sponsors. Our analysis serves readers, not corporate interests.

Accessible Expertise

Whether you're a casual fan, motorsport enthusiast, industry professional, or investor, our analysis is written to be clear without oversimplifying complex business dynamics.

Regular Updates

Motorsport business moves fast. We publish weekly analysis and break critical news as it develops.
