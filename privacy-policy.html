Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to iomotorsport. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your data is collected and used. This Privacy Policy applies to our website at iomotorsport.com and all related services, including our endurance racing coverage, esports team content, race predictions, and blog articles.

By using our website, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our website.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways to provide you with the best possible experience:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Email addresses when you contact us through our contact form or subscribe to updates.
  • User Content: Any comments, feedback, or content you voluntarily submit to our website.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, and screen resolution.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, and navigation paths.
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address (country/region level only).
  • Referral Information: The website or source that referred you to iomotorsport.

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality and security.
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics (GA4) cookies to understand site usage and improve our content.
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense cookies to deliver relevant advertisements and measure ad performance.
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings and preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Content Delivery: To provide you with motorsport news, race predictions, esports coverage, and blog content.
  • Site Improvement: To analyze user behavior and improve our website's performance, design, and content.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense.
  • Analytics: To generate aggregate statistics about our audience and content performance.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics 4: For website analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google Ads Policy
  • Google Fonts: For typography. Fonts are loaded from Google servers.

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data in these situations:

  • Service Providers: With Google for analytics and advertising services as described above.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request.
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of iomotorsport, our users, or others.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Cookie Management: You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Ad Personalization: Manage your ad preferences at Google Ads Settings.
  • Data Access/Deletion: Contact us to request access to or deletion of your personal data.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

Our website is operated from the United States. If you are accessing our site from outside the US, please be aware that your data may be transferred to and processed in the US, where data protection laws may differ from your country.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website. Your continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@iomotorsport.com
  • Contact Form: iomotorsport.com/contact
