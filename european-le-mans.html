About European Le Mans Series

Founded by the Automobile Club de l'Ouest (ACO), the European Le Mans Series is a premier platform for teams aspiring to compete at the 24 Hours of Le Mans. With races across Europe's most iconic circuits, ELMS delivers world-class prototype and GT racing.
