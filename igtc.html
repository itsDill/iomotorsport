Championship Overview

🌍 Global Challenge

  • 5 continents, 5 legendary circuits
  • February to November season
  • Manufacturer championship focus
  • Pro driver combinations
  • GT3 Balance of Performance

🏎️ GT3 Specifications

  • Production-based GT cars
  • ~500-600 hp (BoP regulated)
  • All major manufacturers
  • Factory team competition
  • Customer racing eligible

🏁 Race Formats

  • 8-24 hour races
  • Multi-driver lineups (3-4 drivers)
  • Day/night racing at most rounds
  • Strategic fuel/tire management
  • Driver change requirements

2025 Race Calendar

February 2

🇦🇺 Bathurst 12 Hour

Mount Panorama Circuit

Season opener at Australia's iconic mountain circuit. The challenging 6.2km layout features the legendary Conrod Straight and Mountain section. Sunrise racing through the Esses is unforgettable.

Duration: 12 Hours
Circuit Length: 6.213 km
Elevation Change: 174 meters
July 26-27

🇧🇪 Total 24 Hours of Spa

Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps

The crown jewel of GT3 racing. Through the Ardennes forest with legendary corners like Eau Rouge, Raidillon, and Blanchimont. Often featuring 60+ car grids and unpredictable weather.

Duration: 24 Hours
Circuit Length: 7.004 km
Expected Grid: 60-70 cars
August 24

🇯🇵 Suzuka 10 Hours

Suzuka Circuit

Japan's figure-eight circuit challenges drivers with high-speed corners and elevation changes. The only remaining FIA-grade circuit with an overpass. Home advantage for Honda, Nissan, and Lexus.

Duration: 10 Hours
Circuit Length: 5.807 km
Layout: Figure-8 with overpass
October 11

🇺🇸 Indianapolis 8 Hour

Indianapolis Motor Speedway

GT3s on the Brickyard. Using the infield road course combined with part of the famous oval banking. American manufacturers push hard at this showcase event for domestic racing.

Duration: 8 Hours
Circuit Length: 4.014 km (road course)
Features: Infield + partial oval
November 22

🇿🇦 Kyalami 9 Hour

Kyalami Grand Prix Circuit

Season finale at South Africa's high-altitude circuit. Thin air affects power and cooling. Championship often decided here under African sunset. Growing event with strong local support.

Duration: 9 Hours
Circuit Length: 4.522 km
Altitude: 1,460 meters

Championship Structure

🏆 Manufacturer Championship

Points awarded to manufacturers based on top finishing cars of each brand at every round.

Scoring: 25-18-15-12-10-8-6-4-2-1 (Top 10)

Best 4 of 5 rounds count toward championship

👥 Driver Requirements

  • Minimum 3 drivers per car
  • Driver rating restrictions apply
  • Minimum drive time per driver
  • Maximum consecutive stint limits
  • FIA International license required

🎯 Entry Categories

  • Overall: Top GT3 teams
  • Pro-Am: Mixed professional/amateur
  • Silver Cup: Silver-rated drivers
  • Am Cup: Bronze/amateur drivers
