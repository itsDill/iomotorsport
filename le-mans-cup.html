Car Specifications

LMP3 Prototypes

Engine: 5.6L Nissan V8
Power: ~460 hp
Weight: 930 kg minimum
Top Speed: ~280 km/h
Manufacturers: Ligier, Duqueine, ADESS

GT3 Cars

Engine: Various (manufacturer-specific)
Power: ~500-600 hp (BoP regulated)
Weight: ~1,300 kg (BoP regulated)
Top Speed: ~300 km/h
Manufacturers: Porsche, Ferrari, Lamborghini, McLaren, etc.

How to Compete

💰 Budget

LMP3 Season: €250,000-400,000 (car lease + operations)

GT3 Season: €300,000-500,000 (car lease + operations)

Most affordable prototype racing in Europe

📋 Requirements

  • FIA International C license minimum
  • Bronze or Silver driver rating
  • Previous racing experience
  • Team entry (turnkey available)
