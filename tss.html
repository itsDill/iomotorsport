About Thailand Super Series

Thailand Super Series (TSS) is the premier motorsport championship in Thailand, organized by Racing Spirit Co Ltd. Since its inception in 2013, TSS has grown to become one of Southeast Asia's most prestigious racing series, attracting competitors from across Thailand and international teams from around the Asia-Pacific region.

The championship features multiple racing categories from GT3 supercars to touring cars, offering thrilling wheel-to-wheel action at world-class circuits including Chang International Circuit in Buriram and Bangsaen Street Circuit in Chonburi.

Racing Categories

GT3 Class

The premier class featuring GT3-spec supercars from manufacturers like Lamborghini, Ferrari, Porsche, Mercedes-AMG, and Audi with professional and amateur drivers.

GTM Class

Modified GT cars with technical regulations slightly below GT3, including Porsche 991 Cup cars, providing exciting racing at more accessible costs.

GTC Class

Single-make cup cars and highly modified sports cars, creating a competitive environment for semi-professional and serious amateur drivers.

Thailand Super Car Class 2

Modified production-based sports cars with limited modifications, offering exciting close racing for developing drivers.

Super Compact

Modified compact cars that showcase the engineering excellence of Thai race teams, featuring highly tuned Honda Civic, Toyota Altis, and other compact car platforms.

Super Pickup

Modified pickup trucks showcasing Thailand's popular vehicle segment with race-prepared Toyota Hilux and Isuzu D-Max trucks competing wheel-to-wheel.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I buy tickets for TSS events?

Tickets for Thailand Super Series events can be purchased through the official Thailand Super Series website or through the circuit's own ticketing platforms. For major events like the Bangsaen Grand Prix, tickets are also available through Thai Ticket Major outlets nationwide.

Are there hospitality packages available?

Yes, premium hospitality packages are available for TSS events, offering VIP access, catered food and beverages, air-conditioned viewing areas, and sometimes paddock access. These can be purchased through the official TSS website or by contacting the series organizers directly.

How can I become a driver in Thailand Super Series?

To compete in TSS, you need to hold a valid racing license from the Royal Automobile Association of Thailand (RAAT) or an equivalent international license recognized by the FIA. New drivers typically start in lower categories like Super Compact or Thailand Super Car Class 2 before progressing to higher categories. Contact Racing Spirit Co Ltd, the series organizers, for specific entry requirements and registration procedures.

Are TSS events broadcast on TV or streaming platforms?

Yes, Thailand Super Series events are broadcast live on Thai television channels and are also available via live streaming on the official TSS Facebook page and YouTube channel. International viewers can watch via the series' official streaming platforms with English commentary available for GT3 and GTM races.
