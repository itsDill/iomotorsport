MotoGP Thailand 2026: Your Ultimate Travel Package Guide

The roar of MotoGP engines returns to the Land of Smiles in 2026! Chang International Circuit in Buriram will once again host the world's premier motorcycle racing championship, and we're here to help international visitors make the most of this incredible opportunity to combine world-class motorsports with Thailand's legendary hospitality.

Why Choose Thailand for MotoGP?

Unlike traditional European venues, MotoGP Thailand offers exceptional value, luxury accommodations at reasonable prices, incredible local cuisine, cultural experiences, and some of the friendliest fans in the world. Plus, you can easily extend your trip to explore Bangkok, island hop, or experience Thai culture.

‚úàÔ∏è Getting to Thailand

International Flights

Most international visitors fly into Bangkok's Suvarnabhumi (BKK) or Don Mueang (DMK) airports. Major airlines offer direct flights from:

  • Europe: 11-13 hours from London, Paris, Frankfurt
  • Australia: 7-9 hours from Sydney, Melbourne
  • USA: 15-18 hours (with connections)
  • Asia: 2-6 hours from major regional cities

Bangkok to Buriram

Multiple convenient options to reach the circuit:

  • Domestic Flight: 1.5 hours, $60-100
  • High-Speed Train: 4 hours, $15-25 (scenic route!)
  • Private Transfer: 5 hours, $150-200
  • Tour Bus: 5 hours, $40-60

Pro Tip: Book Early!

Accommodation fills up quickly during MotoGP weekend. Book 6-8 months in advance for best selection and prices. Many hotels offer racing packages with shuttle service, breakfast, and circuit transfers.
