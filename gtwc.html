About GT World Challenge

The GT World Challenge is SRO Motorsports Group's flagship GT3 racing series, operating across three continents. Featuring identical technical regulations worldwide, GTWC showcases the finest GT racing with manufacturer-backed teams and privateer squads competing in some of the world's most prestigious endurance events, including the legendary Total 24 Hours of Spa.
