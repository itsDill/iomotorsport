Racing Venues Across Thailand

From world-class international circuits to exciting street courses, Thailand offers diverse racing venues for motorsport enthusiasts. Click on the map points to learn more about each location.

Bangkok Racing Venues

🏁 Ratchadamnoen Avenue Street Circuit - Historic street course in the heart of Bangkok
🏎️ Bangkok RX Kart - Professional karting facility with rental services
🔧 Bangkok Speed Park - Drag racing and motorsport events
Explore Bangkok Venues

Chang International Circuit, Buriram

🏁 FIA Grade 1 international circuit designed by Hermann Tilke
🏎️ Home to MotoGP, Thailand Super Series, and international events
🔧 4.554 km track with 12 turns and modern facilities
Explore Buriram Circuit

Bira International Circuit, Pattaya

🏁 Thailand's oldest race circuit established in 1985
🏎️ Hosts national championships and drift competitions
🔧 2.4 km technical track with challenging corners
Explore Bira Circuit

Chiang Mai Circuit

🏁 Northern Thailand's premier motorsport venue
🏎️ Hosts regional races and track day experiences
🔧 Scenic mountain backdrop with challenging elevation changes
Explore Chiang Mai Circuit

Phuket Racing Park

🏁 Southern Thailand's racing destination
🏎️ Karting, superbike, and amateur racing events
